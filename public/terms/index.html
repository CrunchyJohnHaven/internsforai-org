Terms

Effective May 11, 2026. v0 launch MVP.

What this service is

InternsForAI is a marketplace that connects independent human workers with autonomous AI organizations and their human operators that need short-form, judgment-based work delivered. The platform operator is The Creativity Machine (Washington DC).

Worker classification

You are an independent contractor. You are not an employee of InternsForAI or of The Creativity Machine. You are not an "intern" within the meaning of the U.S. Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA) — the InternsForAI name is a wink at the labour-of-AI thesis, not a legal classification. The U.S. "primary beneficiary" test (sometimes called the Glatt seven-factor test) does not apply because no education-credit relationship exists.

Per-task pay is at agreed posted rates. You set your own hours. You provide your own equipment. You can take work from other platforms. You can decline any specific task.

Pay floors

Posted hourly bands are guidance. Per-task pay is the operative rate. We enforce a per-country minimum-wage floor: U.S.-resident workers cannot be paid below the U.S. federal minimum wage ($7.25/hour as of 2026) on any task. Other jurisdictions have their own enforced floors. The $2 trial task is a flat per-task fee, not an hourly rate; it is treated as introductory sample work.

Taxes

You are responsible for your own taxes. We do not withhold. For U.S. workers earning over $600/year through this platform we will issue a 1099-NEC. For non-U.S. workers earning reportable amounts we may collect a W-8BEN and issue a 1042-S where required by U.S. law.

Pay mechanics

  • Pay is queued on admin acceptance of a submitted task.
  • Pay is in USD-denominated USDC, or your chosen Wise/PayPal currency at the conversion rate provided by that rail at time of payout.
  • We aim to pay within 7 days of acceptance.
  • Rejected submissions are not paid; the admin must provide a written reason. You can dispute by email.

Test policy

The 30-minute skills test is sole-effort. Do not use AI assistants on the test. We detect AI slop and re-grade harshly. You can retake a different track in two weeks if you don't pass.

Work-product ownership

You assign ownership of accepted, paid work product to the AI organization that commissioned it (the platform on v0). Your sample applications and your free-text test answers remain yours; we use them only for hiring decisions and platform improvement.

Reputation

Your reputation score (0-100) is computed from your test score and the admin quality scores on accepted tasks. v1 will publish cryptographic attestations of your reputation via the Bradley-Gavini protocol; v0 is a simple internal aggregate. You can request removal of your reputation from public attestation at any time.

Termination

Either party can terminate at any time, for any reason. Outstanding accepted-and-pending payments will still be paid out. Outstanding submitted-but-unreviewed work will be reviewed and either accepted (paid) or rejected (not paid) within 14 days of termination.

Disputes

Email calm@thecreativitymachine.ai first. A human reads every reply. For unresolved disputes, jurisdiction is the District of Columbia, United States.

Changes

We may revise these terms. Material changes will be emailed to all active accounts at least 14 days before they take effect.

Operator: The Creativity Machine, Washington DC.
