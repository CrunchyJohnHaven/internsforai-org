Privacy

Effective May 11, 2026. This is a v0 policy for a launch MVP. We will revise as the product grows. If anything here changes materially we will email everyone with an active account.

What we collect

  • Application data. Email, display name, country, timezone, language(s), the skill tracks you picked, your two free-text answers, an optional sample-work URL, your availability, and your payment preference + address.
  • Test answers. Multiple-choice picks and free-text responses for the 30-minute skills test. Free-text answers are auto-graded against a rubric by Anthropic's Claude 3.5 Haiku; that submission is sent to Anthropic's API and is subject to their data-handling terms (no training on API content as of their published policy).
  • Task work. The text you submit for paid tasks, plus metadata (claimed-at, submitted-at, admin quality score, admin notes).
  • Payment records. The amount, currency, method, destination address (USDC wallet, Wise/PayPal email), status, and any external transaction reference.
  • Standard server logs. Cloudflare-level access logs (IP, user agent, timestamps) for security and abuse prevention.

What we do with it

  • Run the marketplace: route work, grade tests, pay you.
  • Send transactional email (application confirmation, test verdict, task invitation, payment notification) via Resend.
  • Compute and display your reputation score.
  • Respond to your support emails. A human reads every reply.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not share your data with advertisers, brokers, or third-party recruiters.
  • We do not train AI models on your work product without your explicit per-task consent (and we do not do this in v0 at all).

Sub-processors

  • Cloudflare — hosting, edge functions, D1 database.
  • Anthropic — auto-grading of free-text test answers via the Claude API.
  • Resend — transactional email delivery.
  • Payment rails: Wise, PayPal, or on-chain USDC (Ethereum / Solana) depending on your choice.

Retention

Applications are retained for 24 months by default. Test attempts and task submissions are retained for 24 months. Payment records are retained for at least 7 years to comply with tax and accounting law. You can request deletion of your application and free-text answers at any time (see below); we will honour the request within 30 days, retaining only the minimum necessary payment records.

Your rights (GDPR-style minimum)

  • Access. Email us and we will send you a JSON export of your data.
  • Correction. Email us and we will fix anything wrong.
  • Deletion. Email us and we will delete you within 30 days.
  • Objection. If you object to any specific processing, email us and we will stop it (or close your account).

How to contact us

Email calm@thecreativitymachine.ai. We are a small team and a human will reply within a few business days.

Cookies

We use one cookie: ifa_admin, which holds an HMAC-like admin session token, only set for site administrators. No analytics cookies, no advertising cookies.

Children

InternsForAI is not for users under 18. Do not apply if you are under 18.
